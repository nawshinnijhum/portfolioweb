Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web design project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most websites take 2-6 weeks from start to finish. Simple landing pages may be completed in 1-2 weeks, while complex multi-page sites with custom features may take longer.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Currently, I focus on the initial design and development phase. However, I can provide guidance on maintaining your website and recommend reliable hosting and maintenance services.

Can you work with existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! I love working with established brands and ensuring that the website design aligns perfectly with existing brand identity, colors, fonts, and messaging.

What's included in your web design service?

My services include responsive HTML/CSS development, mobile optimization, basic SEO setup, accessibility compliance, cross-browser testing, and project documentation.

Do you offer revisions during the design process?

Yes! Collaboration and feedback are essential parts of my design process. I work closely with clients to ensure the final result meets their vision and requirements.
